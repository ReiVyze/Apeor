Empowering Policy Decision-Making with OracleE

OracleE is a state-of-the-art sentiment analysis platform designed specifically for government consultation modules. By leveraging advanced Natural Language Processing, we transform raw public feedback into actionable intelligence, helping policymakers understand the pulse of the citizens with surgical precision.

🎯
Sentiment Precision
Identify positive, neutral, and negative sentiment across thousands of comments instantly using RoBERTa and DistilBert models.
📊
Policy-Centric Analytics
Map feedback directly to specific policy clauses and regions to identify localized concerns or specific clause friction.
🔒
Enterprise Security
Built with government-grade security standards, ensuring citizen data is anonymized and processed with strict encryption protocols.

The OracleE Mission

Our mission is to bridge the gap between citizen voices and legislative action. By providing a transparent, data-driven view of public opinion, we enable a more participatory and responsive democratic process.
